## Project Overview

This project analyzes Snack Attack Shack’s business performance across revenue streams, customer loyalty, product lifecycle, and operational efficiency. The dataset spans July 2022 to April 2024, excluding partial May 2024 records to ensure time‑based accuracy. The outcome is a structured report providing actionable insights for profitability, inventory management, and strategic growth.

## Objectives

- Identify top customers, products, and categories driving revenue (Pareto/80‑20 analysis)  
- Assess customer loyalty patterns (new, non‑returning, dormant, regular)  
- Evaluate product lifecycle and discontinued items  
- Analyze inventory risks (stockouts, excess inventory, reorder triggers)  
- Compare shipping carrier performance (speed vs. cost)  
- Forecast demand and profitability trends  

## Methodology

- Data cleaning and preparation in Google Sheets  
- Exploratory analysis of revenue, customers, products, and categories  
- Segmentation of customer loyalty classes using purchase frequency and recency  
- Product lifecycle and price point analysis  
- Inventory and shipping carrier evaluation  
- AI‑assisted forecasting for demand and profitability  

## Key Findings

- **Revenue Concentration:** 25 customers (28% of base) generated 69.6% of total revenue  
- **Product Portfolio:** Top 25 products (32% of catalog) accounted for 68.2% of revenue  
- **Categories:** Beverages, Dairy, and Confections contributed 53.1% of revenue  
- **Customer Loyalty:** Regular customers drove 96.8% of revenue; dormant customers remain a reactivation opportunity  
- **Inventory Risks:** Multiple products flagged for urgent reorder; discontinued items still in excess stock  
- **Shipping:** Speedy Express fastest (8.6 days avg), United Package most cost‑effective (€8.76 avg)  
- **Forecasting:** Profitability trends highlight €10–40 price range as optimal for demand and revenue
